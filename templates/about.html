FAQ

Why Should I choose Medical Health

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced doctors dedicated to providing the best medical care. Our modern facilities, advanced technology, and patient-centered approach ensure you receive the highest quality treatment. We are committed to your health and wellbeing every step of the way.

What are the Centre’s visiting hours?

Our clinic is open Saturday to Tuesday from 8am to 10pm. Emergency services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to minimize waiting time, though walk-ins are always welcome during working hours.

How many visitors are allowed?

To ensure a comfortable and safe environment for all patients, we allow a maximum of 2 visitors per patient at a time. Visitors must be 12 years or older. Special exceptions can be made for children patients or critical care situations upon request from the medical staff.
